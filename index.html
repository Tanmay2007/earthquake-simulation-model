Working Principle

A DC motor with an offset weight generates vibrations that simulate an earthquake. Two structures are placed on the platform — one normal and one with base isolation. The isolation layer reduces vibration through damping.

Components Used

  • Arduino Uno
  • Motor Driver
  • DC Motor
  • LCD Display
  • Rubber/Springs
  • Battery

Observation

The normal structure experiences greater vibration, while the base-isolated structure remains more stable due to reduced energy transfer.
